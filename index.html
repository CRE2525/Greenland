AWESOME ANIMALS

We call Greenland Kalaallit Nunaat, the Greenlanders’ Land. However, in reality it belongs just as much to the wildlife. The world’s largest island contains a wealth of fascinating species of animal that have all adapted to the Arctic climate both on land and in water.

SUPER SCENERY

The National Park in Greenland is an Arctic paradise and a wilderness with wildlife that cannot be matched in the inhabited areas of the country. Located in the north-eastern corner of the country, the park is larger than any other national park in the world.

COOL CULTURE

The first people to set foot in Greenland arrived 4000 to 5000 years ago from the North American continent via Canada when the sea froze in the narrow strait at Thule in northern Greenland.
